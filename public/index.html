How to Play

Desktop Controls:

  • Left click - Reveal a cell
  • Right click - Flag/unflag a cell as a mine
  • Double click - Mass open adjacent cells (when flags match the number)

Mobile Controls:

  • Tap - Reveal a cell
  • Long press - Flag/unflag a cell as a mine
  • Double tap - Mass open adjacent cells (when flags match the number)

Rules:

  • Goal: Reveal all cells that don't contain mines
  • Numbers show how many mines are adjacent to that cell
  • Flags help you mark cells you think contain mines
